# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 May 2026

ScaleRoulette is a violin scale practice app. It does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Stored on Your Device

ScaleRoulette stores the following settings locally on your device:

- selected grade
- selected theme
- practice filters
- Easy/Normal/Hard mode
- avoid-repeats setting
- scales marked Easy or Hard

This information stays on your device. It is used only to make the app remember your preferences.

## Network, Analytics, and Advertising

ScaleRoulette does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party login services.

## Children's Privacy

ScaleRoulette is suitable as a practice aid for students, teachers, and parents. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Syllabus Data

ScaleRoulette contains violin scale and arpeggio prompts extracted from the ABRSM Bowed Strings Practical Grades syllabus from 2024. The app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by ABRSM. Users should cross-check the official syllabus before relying on any prompt for exam preparation.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in this public GitHub repository or use the contact details listed on the repository owner's GitHub profile.
